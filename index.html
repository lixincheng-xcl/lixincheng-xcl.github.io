Research Topics

My research interests focus on developing machine learning methods to understand and depict real large-scale unstructured data, and to generate and create synthetic data for simulation and applications.

Specifically, my research topics center on Machine Learning and Computer Vision, especially in:

  • Generative AI models: Image/Video generation/editing; Multimodal Large Language Models
  • Human-centric AI: Foundation and human-centric pre-training, perception, understanding and reasoning
  • Self-supervised / un-supervised / semi-supervised learning
  • Object/Attribute detection/recognition; Image/Text-to-image retrieval
