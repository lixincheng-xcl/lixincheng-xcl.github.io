Research Topics

My research focuses on designing machine learning algorithms to understand and depict real large-scale unstructured data, and to generate and create synthetic data for simulation and applications.

Specifically, my research topics center on Machine Learning and Computer Vision, especially in:

  • Generative AI: image/video generation and editing
  • Foundation model pre-training and human-centric representation learning
  • Self-supervised, unsupervised, and semi-supervised learning
  • Object and attribute detection, recognition, and image/text retrieval
